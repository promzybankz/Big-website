Who we are

Who we are

Binary Generation Labs is a training institute committed to training and empowering young minds, students, schools and Individuals in STEM and tech skills by creating coding/STEM clubs and centres in secondary schools and other locations across africa.

We believe every tech enthusiast should start learning as early as possible.

We have a goal of training 3000 students continously by 2023 through collaboration with goverment bodies, private and other organisations.

Our Vision and Mission

Our Vision

To train a generation of young passionate technological inclined individuals that willwith their acquired skills change Africafor better by building world-classsolutions.

Our Mission

To expose students and every individual to problem-solving skills designed to increase their innovation and productivity by creating coding/STEM clubs in schools and other locations.

Our Scope

Our Scope

We organize STEM/coding club centres to give students in-depth knowledge of these fields, having critically examined that these particular courses are indeed beginners friendly and suitable to train young minds.

Scratch Programming

Scratch is a free programming language and our students have created their own interactive stories, games, and animations.

Python Programming

Python is an easy to learn programming language very similar to english language so suitable for students

Web Development

Website development is an advance skills, students are taught to build professional websites.

Graphics Design

Graphic design is a craft where students create visual content to communicate messages.

Robotics

Roboics is a skill with which students are introduced to building real life robots they can control.
